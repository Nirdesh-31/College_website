Curriculum


UA offers 10+2 program in Science, Commerce and Humanities Stream. The students are taught by our highly qualified teachers.

Following are the curriculum and college timing of each of the streams


Science Stream 6:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. (morning shift)
10.55 am to 5.20 pm (day shift)
Management Stream 6:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. (morning shift)
11:40 a. m. to 4:50 p.m. (day shift)
Humanities Stream 6:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. (morning shift)

  • Biology (Botany + Zoology)

  • Computer Science
