Evaluation Scheme


UA has designed an evaluation system of its own. In order to assess student progress, UA will hold one Unit test, two Saturday tests, and three Terminal Examinations. Class tests are held regularly in each subject. The final evaluation of students consists of the following criteria:



Progress Evaluation

In order to evaluate the comprehension and expression of subject texts of students, UA holds unit and class tests in all subjects, Mock Examinations before Second Terminal and Send-up Examinations and three Terminal Examinations. Second and Send-up Re-Examinations are again held for those who do not clearly pass their examinations to appear in the NEB examination.

Practical examination

Practical revision classes are held for the NEB Final Practical Exam to ensure the success of students of Science (Physics, Computer Science, Chemistry, and Biology), Computer Science, Marketing and Hotel Management; and Sociology, Mass Communication and Population Studies of Humanities.
